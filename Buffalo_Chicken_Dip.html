Buffalo Chicken Dip

by Grace Tenny

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo Sauce (use more if desired)
  • 2 cups Shredded Chicken (1 whole rotisserie chicken is best)
  • 2 cups Shredded Cheese
  • Minced Garlic

Directions

Step 1: In mixing bowl, combine softened cream cheese, ranch dressing, sour cream, garlic, chicken, buffalo sauce, and 1 cup of cheese. Stir until combined well.

Step 2: Lightly spray slow cooker with non-stick cooking sprat or use slow cooker bags. Transfer the mixture to the slow cooker and top with remaining cheese.

Step 3: Cover and cook on LOW for 2-3 hours, until hot and bubbly. Make sure you stir occassionally. Top with green onions. Serve hot with tortilla chips.

Step 4: Enjoy!!!
