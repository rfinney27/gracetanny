Butter Bean & Chorizo Stew

by Eddie Estrada

Ingredients

  • 200g Cooking Chorizo
  • 2 x 400g Canned Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 x 400g Canned Butter Beans
  • 130g Pesto

Directions

Step 1: Slice the chorizo and tip into a large sauce pan over a medium heat.

Step 2: Fry for 5 min. or until the chorizo starts to turn dark brown.

Step 3: Add tomatoes and butter beans.

Step 4: Bring to a boil and simmer for 10min.

Step 4: Swirl through the pesto, season to taste with salt and serve.
